NetSuite Integration
Scope This Early

Great news — Shopify has a well-established path for NetSuite. There are two main approaches depending on complexity:

Official Shopify App NetSuite Connector by Shopify

Built and maintained by Shopify. Handles the core data flows out of the box with minimal configuration — the fastest path to go live for standard workflows.

iPaaS Middleware Celigo, Boomi, or MuleSoft

More flexible and customizable. Best for complex workflows, custom field mappings, or multi-system environments. Celigo is the most common choice for NetSuite + Shopify.

What the integration covers:
📦 Product & catalog sync (NetSuite → Shopify)
📊 Real-time inventory levels from NetSuite
🛒 Orders pushed to NetSuite as sales orders
👤 Bi-directional customer & company data
💲 NetSuite price levels → Shopify B2B catalogs
🧾 Invoice & payment term management

Your Platform Story
Where You Are Today

Vault CPS has been running a homegrown e-commerce and ERP platform for nearly 20 years — the same codebase powering both. The business has made the decision to modernize: NetSuite is already live as the financial ERP (as of December 2025), and the next step is replacing the commerce layer with a platform that integrates cleanly and scales with the business. The current system is in "keep the lights on" mode as resources shift toward building the new ecosystem.

The goal is to have a new commerce platform live in 9–12 months, with customers migrated in waves from the legacy system to the new one.
